Welcome to New Zealand

ACTIVITIES

Explore the breathtaking landscapes with activities like hiking the Tongariro Alpine Crossing, bungee jumping in Queenstown, or relaxing in the natural hot springs of Rotorua. Experience the thrill of whale watching in Kaikoura or take a scenic flight over the stunning fjords of Milford Sound.

PLACES

Discover the vibrant culture and history in places like Auckland, Wellington, and Christchurch. Visit historical sites such as the Waitangi Treaty Grounds, the iconic Hobbiton Movie Set, and the majestic landscapes of Fiordland National Park. Each region offers unique landmarks and local experiences.

FOOD & DRINKS

Indulge in New Zealand's culinary delights, including fresh seafood, world-famous lamb, and the delicious pavlova dessert. Don’t miss trying local wines, particularly Sauvignon Blanc from Marlborough and Pinot Noir from Central Otago. Experience traditional Māori cuisine with a hangi feast.
